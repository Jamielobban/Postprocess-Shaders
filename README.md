# Scanners

A collection of post-processing shaders for Unity 6.  
Written in HLSL and Shader Graph.

All shaders are located in `Assets/Shaders/`.

---

## Included Effects

- Binary
- BitCrush
- ChromaticBloom
- ColorQuant
- CompressionBlocks
- DeathStranding
- Dither
- DoomDarkAges Scanner Effect
- GlitchMelt
- Halftone
- Heat
- Kaleidoscope
- LUT
- Mosaic
- NMS Scanner Effect
- Painting
- PaletteSwap
- PixelSort
- Posterize
- Sobel

---

## Requirements

- Unity 6
- Universal Render Pipeline (URP)

---

## Usage

1. Open the project in Unity 6
2. Navigate to `Assets/Shaders/`
3. Assign a shader to a material or post-processing volume
4. Adjust parameters in the Inspector

---

## Notes

- Effects are modular and independent
- Intended for experimentation and visual exploration
- Some effects may be performance-heavy depending on resolution
